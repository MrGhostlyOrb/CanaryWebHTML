Our Facilities

Canary Canoe Club is proud to have one of the best canoe clubhouses in the country. It was refurbished with support from Norwich City Council and a National Lottery grant in 2000 and we're now making further improvements with the help of British Canoeing and Sport England.

The clubhouse has its own riverside launching site and can be seen (downstream on right) whilst standing on Cleveland Bridge. The entrance to the small lane leading to the clubhouse (Cleveland Cottages) is by the Methodist church on the main A4.

The address of the clubhouse is:

Canary Canoe Club , The Old Organ Factory , Cleveland Cottages , Norwich , NR1 5DD
